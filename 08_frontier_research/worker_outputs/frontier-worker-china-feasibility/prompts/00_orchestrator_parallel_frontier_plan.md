# Prompt 00 — Orchestrator Parallel Frontier Plan

## Task
Create the execution plan for a fresh parallel deep-research run on frontier startup ideas.

## Context
This project starts from scratch. Do not use old RIDs, old scoring tables, or old source ledgers. The goal is to find Hinetics-like frontier opportunities in five domains:

1. Power / energy / grid / data-center infrastructure
2. Semiconductor / electronics / advanced packaging
3. Biomedical / medtech / bio-instrumentation
4. Industrial / robotics / advanced manufacturing
5. Extreme / cryogenic / superconducting / high-field / harsh environments

## Source policy
Use `01_sources/` policies. Academic article evidence must be peer-reviewed and moderate/high impact. Do not count arXiv or other preprints. Official policy/regulation/standards/government/national-lab/public-filing/procurement sources may count even without impact factor.

## Deliverable
Produce:

1. Worker plan for Worker 1–7.
2. Exact output contract for worker outputs.
3. Minimum source quota per worker.
4. Minimum candidate quota per worker.
5. Source-audit gate criteria.
6. Merge/dedupe plan.
7. Frontier scoring rubric.
8. Final decision gates.
9. Files that will be written.
10. Warnings to prevent old-research contamination.

## Required quotas
Each domain worker must produce:

- 20–30 fresh candidate ideas.
- 20–30 fresh sources.
- At least 12 peer-reviewed high/moderate-impact academic or top peer-reviewed conference sources where applicable.
- At least 5 official/standards/regulatory/procurement/policy sources where applicable.
- At least 3 rejected ideas with reasons.

Company-radar worker must produce:

- 20 US or US-linked Hinetics-like company/project profiles.
- For each, identify a possible China analogue or adjacent subsystem wedge.

China-feasibility worker must produce:

- China feasibility rules and sector-specific pitfalls.
- No final recommendations yet.

## Do not
Do not score ideas yet. Do not produce final strategy. Do not use old databases.
