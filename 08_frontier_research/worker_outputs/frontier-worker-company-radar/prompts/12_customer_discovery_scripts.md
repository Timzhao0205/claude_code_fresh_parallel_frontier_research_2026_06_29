# Prompt 12 — Customer Discovery Scripts

## Task
For the top 5 R&D pilot tracks, create customer discovery scripts.

## Deliverables

For each top track:

1. Buyer persona.
2. User persona.
3. Economic buyer.
4. Technical evaluator.
5. 20 non-leading interview questions.
6. 10 workflow mapping questions.
7. 10 budget/procurement questions.
8. 10 questions about current alternatives.
9. Strong-pain signals.
10. Weak-pain signals.
11. Interview scoring template.
12. China interview targets.
13. US interview targets.

## Rule

Do not pitch. Do not ask “would you buy this?” Ask about current workflow, costs, failure consequences, procurement, and alternatives.
