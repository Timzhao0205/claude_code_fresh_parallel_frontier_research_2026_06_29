# Prompt 10 — Rank and Red-Team Frontier Candidates

## Task
Score only candidates marked CLEARED or CLEARED_WITH_WEAKNESS by the source audit.

## Rubric: 100 points

- Boundary-shift magnitude: 20
- System-unlock potential: 15
- Pain intensity and willingness to pay: 12
- Prototype path 2026–2028: 10
- Hardware/software/control integration depth: 8
- Defensibility: 8
- China-first wedge: 7
- US expansion path: 6
- Regulatory/export risk, inverted: 6
- Capital efficiency: 4
- Founder-fit after unbiased search: 4

## Deliverables

1. Ranked table of all cleared candidates.
2. Top 25 frontier candidates.
3. Top 12 deep-dive candidates.
4. Top 5 R&D pilot tracks.
5. Red-team failure case for each top 12.
6. Watchlist appendix for high-upside but weak-evidence candidates.
7. CSV-ready rows for `frontier_scored_database.csv`.

## Rules

- Do not score HOLD/KILL/WEAK_SIGNAL_ONLY candidates.
- Do not inflate score because an idea is exciting.
- Explicitly penalize over-cleanroom, over-regulatory, or commodity China traps.
