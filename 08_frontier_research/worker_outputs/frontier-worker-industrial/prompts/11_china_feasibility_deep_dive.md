# Prompt 11 — China Feasibility Deep Dive

## Task
For the top 15 frontier candidates, assess whether each should be China-first, US-first, dual-track, or avoided.

## Deliverables per candidate

1. China-first attractiveness.
2. First Chinese customer segment.
3. First US customer segment.
4. Supply-chain advantage in China.
5. Commodity/copycat risk in China.
6. Regulatory/export-control risk.
7. IP strategy.
8. Local partner requirement.
9. US expansion path.
10. Go/no-go recommendation.

## Labels

Use one:

- China-first attractive
- China-first possible but dangerous
- US-first then China manufacturing
- Dual-track from day one
- Not recommended for China-first

## Rule

Do not recommend copying Hinetics or any company directly. Recommend an adjacent, defensible subsystem wedge.
