## Low-confidence market/TAM aggregators

Generic SEO market-report sites are not counted. They may be used only to identify competitor names and then replaced with primary sources.

## Rule

If a worker uses a blocked source, it must mark the claim as WEAK_SIGNAL_ONLY and find a replacement before the candidate can be scored.
