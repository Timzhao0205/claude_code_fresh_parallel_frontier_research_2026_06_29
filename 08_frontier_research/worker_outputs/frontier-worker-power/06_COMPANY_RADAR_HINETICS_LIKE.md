# 06 — US Hinetics-like Company Radar

Use this as a seed list for Worker 6. Do not copy these companies. Study their pattern: clear extreme metric, hardware identity, and high-end first market.

## Power / electric machines / AI power

- Hinetics — cryogen-free superconducting motor/generator direction.
- H3X — high-power-density integrated motor drives.
- VEIR — superconducting power delivery for data centers.
- Heron Power — medium-voltage solid-state-transformer + SuperBBU for AI data centers.
- DG Matrix / Amperesand / Atom Power — solid-state power conversion/protection.

## Semiconductor / thermal / advanced packaging

- JetCool — microconvective liquid cooling.
- Akash Systems — diamond cooling for AI servers and space/semiconductor devices.
- Diamond Foundry — diamond thermal / electronic materials.
- xLight / Celestial AI / Ayar Labs / Lightmatter — frontier photonics / AI infrastructure.

## Biomedical / intervention / neurotech

- Paradromics — high-data-rate brain-computer interface.
- Synchron — interventional BCI through blood vessels.
- Neuralink — generalized brain interface.
- Vicarious Surgical / Virtual Incision / Medical Microinstruments — robotic surgery boundary shifts.

## Industrial / manufacturing

- Hadrian — AI-powered factories for aerospace/defense production.
- Atomic Industries — AI/manufacturing tooling and mold-making.
- VulcanForms — additive manufacturing production infrastructure.
- Freeform / Seurat / Divergent — advanced manufacturing systems.

## Extreme / fusion / cryogenic / high-field

- Commonwealth Fusion Systems — HTS magnets and SPARC/ARC.
- Thea Energy — planar-coil stellarator architecture and dynamic control.
- Type One Energy — HTS stellarator fusion technology.
- Quaise Energy — millimeter-wave deep drilling for geothermal.
- Xcimer / Realta / Zap / Helion — fusion approaches to study, not necessarily copy.

## Research rule

For every company, classify:

- System boundary they change
- Extreme metric
- First high-end market
- Product vs full system
- China analogue feasibility
- What a small China-first startup could do adjacent to it
- Why direct copying would fail
