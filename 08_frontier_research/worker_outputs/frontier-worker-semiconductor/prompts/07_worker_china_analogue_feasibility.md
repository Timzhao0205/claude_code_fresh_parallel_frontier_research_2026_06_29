# Prompt 07 — Worker China Analogue Feasibility

## Task
Research which frontier startup directions are feasible as China-first or China-early companies.

## Domains

- Power / AI data-center infrastructure
- Semiconductor / advanced packaging / thermal
- Biomedical / high-end medtech
- Industrial / robotics / advanced manufacturing
- Extreme / HTS / cryogenic / nuclear / fusion / geothermal

## Deliverables

1. China feasibility matrix by domain.
2. High-potential China-first wedges.
3. China commodity traps.
4. US-expansion barriers.
5. Export-control / dual-use risks.
6. IP / copycat risks.
7. Recommended China-first vs US-first vs dual-track labels.
8. 30 customer discovery targets in China.
9. 30 customer discovery targets in the US for comparison.
10. CSV-ready source rows.

## Rules

Do not recommend direct clones. Recommend adjacent subsystem wedges where China has supply-chain advantage but where defensibility is still possible.
